If you frequently find yourself disorganized, in need of a total index of your life and activities, are looking for a more organic way to share your life than offered by current social media sites, or just want to record your every day actions for posterity, then the Life Tap is the solution for you. It's the first lifelogging tool with extensible capabilities that allow you to capture anything as simple as an online call history to a complete recording of all your technology-related actions. In a sense, it will allow you to spy on yourself

FAQ

Why should I trust you with all my personal data?
Our servers are secure by design and located in a favorable jurisdiction for data privacy.
Addditionally all our software is derived from the open-source Cyber-Yam project, so if you really want total control you can simply use our hardware and apps but deploy your own server on a platfrom under your control.
How will you be making money off of me and my data?
We'll sell you our lifelogging service for $10 a month, and software for your iPhone, Android device, PC and Mac for additional charges. Furthermore, we'll eventually have a line of hardware devices that you can purchase that will allow you to start lifelogging on a 24/7 basis. Finally, we'll be opening our own app store that will allow individual vendors to add 3rd-party extensions to the Life Tap.

If you are OK with us using your personal data (scrubbed of your personal information) for research and advertising purposes, we can offer you a 50% discount on the monthly rate.
How do I contribute money or time?
Right now you can't contribute either, because this web page is actually a project for the Startup Engineering course at Coursera. If there is enough legitimate interest this may change.

If this does sound to you like a great idea that can become a profitable business let me know about it.
